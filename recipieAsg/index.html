Pizza receipe

By Sejal Khatri

Ready to serve pizza within 5 minutes

Serves 2 people

Ingrediants

  • Get ready to bake pizza from the store
  • Tomato Sauce(recommended)
  • Chilli Flakes
  • Oregano

Method

  1. Remove the pizza from the packet
  2. Carefully put it in the Oven
  3. Bake it for 30 mins at 250F temperature
  4. Enjoy the pizza with your loved ones :)
